About us

Welcome to the sonic revolution of LIMP FESTIVAL, where the unbridled power of rock music takes center stage! LIMP, an acronym echoing both rebellion and intensity, sets the stage for a festival experience like no other. Imagine a sprawling outdoor venue pulsating with the raw energy of legendary rock acts and the groundbreaking sounds of emerging talents. LIMP FESTIVAL is not just a celebration of music; it's a vibrant convergence of diverse rock genres, from classic and hard rock to punk, alternative, and indie, ensuring an eclectic auditory journey for every attendee. Our festival grounds transform into a dynamic tapestry of art installations, food stalls, and interactive zones, creating an immersive environment that goes beyond the music. Join us as we embrace the countercultural spirit of rock, fostering an inclusive space for self-expression and shared passion. LIMP FESTIVAL is where the heartbeat of rock meets the pulse of the rebellious soul. Are you ready to amplify the experience?

FAQs

LIMP Festival is an all-ages event. However, attendees under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a legal guardian or an adult over the age of 21. Please bring valid identification, such as a driver's license or passport, to ensure smooth entry.

Outside food and beverages, including alcohol, are not allowed into the festival grounds. We offer a diverse range of food and drink vendors to satisfy your cravings. Stay hydrated and enjoy the delicious offerings from our festival vendors.

We encourage attendees to bring essentials like sunscreen, hats, and comfortable clothing. However, items such as large umbrellas, glass containers, and illegal substances are strictly prohibited. Please refer to our official guidelines for a comprehensive list of allowed and restricted items.
